Who I Am

I'm a systems engineer who lives at the intersection where enterprise mainframes meet modern cloud architecture.

With deep expertise in COBOL, DB2, and mainframe systems, combined with fluency in Python, Docker, and cloud-native technologies, I specialize in the critical work of modernizing legacy enterprise infrastructure without breaking what works.
